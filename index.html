About

I am a PhD candidate in Civil Engineering (Transportation Systems) at the University of Toronto, supervised by Prof. Eric J. Miller. My doctoral research focuses on modelling household automobile transactions for integrated transportation and land use models.

My broader interests include travel demand modelling, electric vehicle adoption, transport decarbonization, ICT and transport, and public transport in the Global South. Before starting my PhD, I completed a master's in Transport and Sustainable Development at École Nationale des Ponts et Chaussées in France and worked in both transport research and civil engineering practice.
